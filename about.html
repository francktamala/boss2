In Business for More than a
Decade

Blue Ocean Security Services (B.O.S.S) is a PSIRA accredited, South African owned Black empowered company which has transformed into one of South Africa's most efficient Security Company's through commitment and service excellence. Since the company's inception in 2003 and B.O.S.S V.I.P Protection Services in 2006, established by a member from the former Mayoral Protection Unit, we have been able to produce innovative and effective security related solutions around South Africa due to our experience.

Our reputation as one of South Africa's most efficient security providers has been obtained through the continuous fulfillment of providing Service Excellence through superior service and customised implementations.

B.O.S.S is a true reflection of the new South African ideal where diverse personnel combine their skill to provide maximum value for our clients. The fact that every employee has committed interest in contributing to the success, growth and prosperity of the company enables us to continuously deliver on our promises and surpass client expectations.

Our Mission

To deliver superior service in all aspects of our business in order to become indispensable to our clients, shareholders and employees through the implementation of the latest technology and guarding available in order to safeguard people, premises and possessions.


Our Vision

To be recognised and respected as the security service provider of choice for both clients and employees.

Our Values

B.O.S.S is committed to the highest standards of ethics and integrity. We are responsible to our clients, to our employees and their families. Our employees are the most valued assets of our company and essential participants with a shared responsibility in fulfilling our mission. We recognise that the quality, motivation and performance of our employees are the key factors in achieving our success. Accordingly, our human Resources policies and practices are built on:

  • Dedication to assisting every employee in reaching his or her full potential in both performance and reward.

  • Commitment to diversity, equal opportunity and fair treatment.

  • Organisational structure and culture which promote employee involvement, open communication, teamwork and co-operation.
